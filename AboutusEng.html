About Us

Khaled Charitable Society for Severe Disabilities (KCS) is a non-profit civil society concerned with persons with moderate and sever disabilities for ages 16 years and above, and strive to provide rehabilitation, entertainment, and integration services, and enable them to obtain their full rights, as this category of disability is completely marginalized and there are no institutions that provide them with the required services and care. Khaled Charitable Society was established on 2022/8/12 in Ramallah Governorate. Registration No. RA-22220018-C

Targeted audience

The organization provides its services to people with severe disabilities aged 16 and above.

Vision

Work to provide a high-level service, rehabilitate and integrate people with severe disabilities in Palestine and enable them to enjoy a dignified life.

Message

Khalid Charity Society provides comprehensive care rehabilitation, recreation and integration services for people with with disabilities in a nurturing and supportive environment that to change their lives by developing their abilities enhancing their independence, improving their quality of life and enhancing their integration into society as it aims to to empower individuals with disabilities through innovative and sustainable solutions that meet their unique needs and positively impact their lives.
