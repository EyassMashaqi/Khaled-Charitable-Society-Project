About Us

Learn about Khaled Charitable Society for Severe Disabilities and our journey in serving the community

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in providing rehabilitation and care services for people with moderate and severe disabilities in Palestine, and to contribute to building an inclusive society that respects the rights of all its members and enables them to participate effectively in social and economic life.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive and distinguished services for people with moderate and severe disabilities, enabling them to obtain their full rights in rehabilitation, care and integration into society through specialized programs that take into account their special needs and respect their human dignity.

Our Values

  • Human dignity and respect for rights
  • Inclusiveness and equity in service delivery
  • Transparency and accountability in work
  • Excellence and innovation in programs
  • Community partnership and cooperation
  • Continuous development and learning

Our History

Khaled Charitable Society for Severe Disabilities was established on December 8, 2022 in Ramallah and Al-Bireh Governorate, with registration number RA-22220018-C. The idea of establishing the association came as a result of the urgent need for specialized services for people with moderate and severe disabilities who lack appropriate services in Palestinian society.

Our Team

We have a team of qualified specialists in the fields of rehabilitation, physiotherapy, occupational therapy, psychology, and social work. We also rely on a network of trained volunteers who contribute to providing services and implementing programs and activities.

Our Core Services

  • Individual and group rehabilitation programs
  • Physical and occupational therapy
  • Recreational and cultural activities
  • Vocational training programs
  • Psychological and social support
  • Family guidance and training
  • Community awareness
  • Community integration programs
