Khaled Society for Severe Disabilities

Khaled Charitable Society is a non-profit civil society organization that focuses on people with moderate and severe disabilities aged 16 years and above. We work to provide rehabilitation, care, entertainment, and integration services, enabling them to obtain their full rights, as this category of disability is completely marginalized.

Our Mission

Providing comprehensive and distinguished services for people with moderate and severe disabilities, enabling them to obtain their full rights in rehabilitation, care, and integration into society through specialized programs that take into account their special needs and respect their human dignity.

Our Services

Individual Rehabilitation

Customized rehabilitation programs for each case according to their special needs

Group Activities

Recreational and educational group activities to enhance social interaction

Family Support

Training and guidance for families to deal with their children's needs

Cognitive Development

Programs to develop cognitive, motor, and social skills

Healthcare

Comprehensive health monitoring in cooperation with specialists

Awareness

Community awareness campaigns to support the rights of people with disabilities
