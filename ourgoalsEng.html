Goals & Services

Learn about our strategic goals and specialized services in serving people with moderate and severe disabilities

Our Strategic Goals

Comprehensive Rehabilitation

Providing comprehensive rehabilitation services for people with moderate and severe disabilities through specialized programs aimed at developing their physical, mental, and social abilities.

Community Integration

Working to integrate people with disabilities into the community through awareness programs and interactive activities that enhance community acceptance and break down social barriers.

Family Support

Providing support and guidance to families of people with disabilities through training and guidance programs to help them properly deal with their children's needs.

Cognitive Development

Developing cognitive and mental abilities through educational and training programs specifically designed to suit each individual's needs and special abilities.

Psychological Care

Providing the necessary psychological and social support to improve the psychological and social condition of people with disabilities and their families.

Community Awareness

Spreading awareness in the community about the rights of people with disabilities and changing negative community perceptions through awareness campaigns and educational activities.

Our Specialized Services

Physical Therapy

  • Assessment and treatment of motor conditions
  • Development of gross motor skills
  • Improving balance and coordination
  • Use of assistive devices
  • Post-injury rehabilitation programs

Occupational Therapy

  • Development of fine motor skills
  • Training in activities of daily living
  • Use of adaptive tools
  • Improving visual-motor coordination
  • Cognitive skills development

Speech & Language Therapy

  • Assessment and treatment of speech disorders
  • Communication skills development
  • Use of alternative communication methods
  • Improving comprehension and expression abilities
  • Interactive communication programs

Recreational Activities

  • Arts and crafts activities
  • Educational and recreational games
  • Music and dance
  • Trips and outings
  • Adapted sports activities

Educational Programs

  • Teaching basic academic skills
  • Interactive learning programs
  • Use of educational technology
  • Literacy programs
  • Life skills development

Vocational Training

  • Assessment of vocational abilities
  • Work skills training
  • Sheltered workshop programs
  • Community work rehabilitation
  • Post-employment follow-up

Our Special Programs

Intensive Rehabilitation Program

A comprehensive 6-month program that includes all rehabilitation and treatment services with intensive individual follow-up for each beneficiary to ensure the best results.

Summer Camp

A two-month summer camp that includes various recreational, educational, and therapeutic activities in a stimulating and fun environment that enhances the comprehensive development of participants.

Home Services Program

Rehabilitation and treatment services at home for cases that cannot reach the center, with family training on how to continue the program.

Family Support Program

A comprehensive program to support and train families of people with disabilities that includes counseling sessions, training workshops, and psychological support groups.

Community Awareness Program

Community awareness campaigns in schools, universities, and institutions to raise awareness about the rights of people with disabilities and the importance of integrating them into society.

Volunteer Program

A program to train and qualify volunteers to participate in providing services and activities, which enhances community partnership in serving this group.
