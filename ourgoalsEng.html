Services

Physiotherapy

Provide physical therapy sessions on a regular basis by evaluating cases, identifying strengths and weaknesses, and developing a suitable plan for each case to maintain and develop their abilities.

Occupational therapy

Providing occupational therapy services on a regular basis by optimizing methods and equipment to work on improving their abilities to perform activities of daily living and developing their skills.

Comprehensive care

Provide healthcare, hygiene, care, and other needs.

Entertainment

Work to provide diverse and appropriate recreational programs in order to enhance their belonging and social participation, and make them feel happy and connected to others.

Objectives of the Society

Providing comprehensive care

Providing comprehensive care by providing rehabilitation, care and integration services for people with disabilities.

Creating a contemporary facility

Create a contemporary, fully-equipped, easily accessible facility tailored to the unique requirements of people with disabilities.

Create communication channels

Establish communication channels with government institutions, civil society, and the business sector to provide the best possible service.

Meeting people's needs

Meeting the needs of individuals with moderate and severe disabilities as the primary beneficiaries of our services and enabling them to meet their needs.

Advocating for disability rights

Defending the rights of people with disabilities and activating their role in society, and informing them of their rights, duties, and ways of working with the rest of society.
